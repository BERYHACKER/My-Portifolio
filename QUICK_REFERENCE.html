‚ùì Common Questions

How do I change my name throughout the portfolio?

Use Find & Replace (Ctrl+H) in your text editor to replace "Beryl" with your name in all HTML files.

How do I add a new project?

Create a new HTML file (like project-name.html), copy the structure from iot-example.html, and add it to the projects section in index.html.

Can I change the colors?

Yes! Edit style-modern.css and replace #00f7ff (cyan) and #78e693 (green) with your desired colors.

How do I make the contact form work?

Use a service like Formspree.io or EmailJS. Check SETUP_GUIDE.md for detailed instructions.
